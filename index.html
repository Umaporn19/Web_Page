Abstract

‚ÄÉ‚ÄÉ‚ÄÉNowadays, car accidents are increasing every day. As a result, the police have to be more stringent about safety measures than before, including collecting forensic evidence which is crucial in investigating car accidents. One such method is photography. Similarly, insurance companies also use photography to detect damage to car parts and analyze the extent of damage, enabling them to evaluate the overall damage to the car. However, this process involves several steps, leading to delays that can cause problems for insurance companies.
  Developers aim to create a "Web Application for car part prediction and damage estimation with single image" to reduce the number of images, time, and steps required to obtain car insurance using AI. The application is trained using car model photography and tested with real car images. First, the car model photographs are input into AI, which learns about car parts and damage. The output is an estimation of the car parts and damage.
  In conclusion, developing the " Web Application for car part prediction and damage estimation with single image" can provide significant benefits to insurance companies by reducing the time and steps required to obtain car insurance. The application streamlines the analysis of car parts and damage, making the process faster and more efficient than before.
